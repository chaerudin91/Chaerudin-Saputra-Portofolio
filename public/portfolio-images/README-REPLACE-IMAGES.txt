Important:
- Put files inside public/portfolio-images/
- In the browser, public files are accessed without the word public.
  Correct: /portfolio-images/page-01.jpg
  Wrong: /public/portfolio-images/page-01.jpg
- Vercel is case-sensitive. page-01.jpg is different from Page-01.JPG.
